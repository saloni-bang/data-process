Demo Project on Data Processing and ETL

This demo project demonstrates the ETL process used to process data from two systems

  • ERP System: Mock of a SAP Enterprise Resource Planning Sytem
  • SRM System: Mock of a SAP Suplier Relationship Management System

The ERP system has details about materials manufactured by a the client ans the sales information of the same. The SRM system on the other hand has procurement details for procuring raw materials or products that are used for manufacturing the materials. Each material has a "Bill of Material" which consists of the products it is made of. The sales of a materials and the prodcurement of products for manfacturing them helps the client estimate the of expenses and profits made by the company.
